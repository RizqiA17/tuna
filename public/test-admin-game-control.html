🐛 Common Issues

❌ Issue: Button shows "Mulai Permainan" after refresh

❌ Issue: Section reverts to "Overview" after refresh

Cause: Section state not properly restored

Fix: Ensure restoreAdminState() restores currentSection

❌ Issue: Team count shows 0 after refresh

Cause: Connected teams data not properly restored

Fix: Ensure WebSocket reconnection loads team data
